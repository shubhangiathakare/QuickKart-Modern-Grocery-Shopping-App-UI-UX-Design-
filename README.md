## **Project Overview**

QuickKart is a clean, intuitive, and user-friendly grocery shopping app. The design focuses on smooth navigation, quick access to products, and a seamless checkout experience.

**Key Features:**

- ✅ Simple product browsing
- ✅ Smart cart & easy checkout
- ✅ Order tracking flow
